About Osprey

Simple, yet functional software aimed at camera clubs, who just want it to work.

Photography is inherently digital now; clubs have their own laptops and projectors. As a member of clubs, and having visited many clubs has convinced me that there is a space for effective programs. Being a Software Engineer as well has put me in good place to fill this gap.

This project is just making in first venture into the wider-world, it's being tested in my own club. Please get in touch if you want to use any of these applications!

Osprey Knockout

Head-to-head image knockout - makes a great fun competition

Osprey Slideshow

Quick and simple slideshows - for when you just want to see images

Osprey Exhibition

Allow visitors to interact with your exhibtion

Osprey Critque

Collaborative image critiquing - in the planning phase.
